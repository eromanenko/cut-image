If the PDF has an odd number of pages, the last page is ignored.

Modes: Switch between Free Form (pencil icon) — each card has individually adjustable corners — and Rectangular (square icon) — all cards share the same fixed width, height, and skew.

Save/Load: Use the Disk icon to export all layout coordinates to a .ini file. Use the Upload icon to restore coordinates. Layouts are auto-saved per filename and restored when re-opening a file.

Instruction: Upload a scanned image or PDF. Click and drag the red corners to adjust borders, or drag the whole card from the center.

Draw Shape: Click the Lasso icon to manually draw custom polygons. Click to add points, press Enter to close the shape, or Esc to cancel.

Auto-Detect: Open Settings (gear icon) to specify expected card sizes in mm for highly accurate detection.

Navigation: Tab cycles through corners. Enter jumps between cards. Arrow Keys move points (Ctrl = 10px steps).

Zoom Window: + / - adjusts magnification. Z toggles zoom window. Rectangle Mode: All cards are identical parallelograms defined by global Width, Height, and Skew (px) values above.

Add card: Click empty area on canvas — a new card appears at the click position. Drag to reposition.

Move: Drag any card. Arrow Keys for precise 1px steps (Ctrl = 10px steps).

Rotate: Press / (0.05° CW) or Shift+/ (0.05° CCW). Enter / Shift+Enter to jump between cards.

Zoom Window: Shows all 4 corners simultaneously. Align each corner to the yellow frame. + / - to adjust magnification.
